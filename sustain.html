Conservation Efforts

To combat species extinction, conservation organizations, governments, and local communities work together to:

Protect habitats: Establish protected areas, restore degraded habitats, and promote sustainable land-use practices.

Monitor populations: Track population sizes and trends to inform conservation efforts.

Control invasive species: Eradicate non-native species that threaten native ecosystems.

Educate and engage: Raise awareness about endangered species and involve local communities in conservation efforts.
